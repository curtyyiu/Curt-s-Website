Introduction

Hi! I'm Curt Adrian J. Aninao, a 16-year-old student who was born in the Philippines and raised in Brunei Darussalam for the majority of my life. I currently study at Immaculate Conception College of Balayan Incorporated. My favorite food is lasagna, and I love science, math, and English!

Hobbies & Skills

I have a passion for performing arts, especially theater and singing. I'm also into dancing and learning new instruments. Currently, I'm a beginner with the piano and plan on getting a guitar soon! I'm also very interested in academics, particularly in science, math, and English.

More About Me

I'm passionate about the creative process and love bringing stories to life on stage. I plan to move to London next year to pursue even more opportunities for growth and exploration in the performing arts and beyond. I dream of seeing the aurora borealis one day!
